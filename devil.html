If I Told You I Was the Devil

If I told you I was the Devil, would you believe me?

The one who stares back at you from the cracks in your soul.

The shadow that knows all your secrets and never blinks.

Some scars don’t heal — they just learn how to speak without bleeding.

I’m not here to steal your soul, but to remind you it’s already mine.

Whispers in the night, the echo of your mistakes.

The fire you refuse to put out because you love the burn.

So, if I told you I was the Devil, would you run, or would you finally listen?

This is the raw truth, unapologetic and bare.
