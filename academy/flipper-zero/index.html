Flipper Zero Mastery

Master the Ultimate Multi-Tool for Hardware Hackers

Course Overview

The Flipper Zero is more than just a portable multi-tool—it's a gateway into the world of hardware hacking, RF security, and physical penetration testing. This comprehensive course will take you from unboxing to advanced exploitation.

You'll learn to interact with RFID systems, analyze and replay Sub-GHz signals, control infrared devices, execute BadUSB attacks, and interface with hardware through GPIO. Each lesson includes working scripts you can copy and deploy immediately.

  • A Flipper Zero device (required for hands-on practice)
