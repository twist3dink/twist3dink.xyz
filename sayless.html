Some scars don’t heal—they just learn how to speak without bleeding.
Silence is a language. Some of us are fluent.
You call it damaged. I call it data.
The system wasn’t built for me to survive it.
I never bit the apple. I hacked the tree.
Pain taught me the algorithm.
They called it madness. I called it the source code.
Not broken. Reformatted.
I stopped praying. Started coding.
Say less. Feel everything.
